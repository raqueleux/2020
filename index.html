Hello!

I am Raquel

My name is Raquel Burroughs and I am a designer. I’ve always appreciated the arts and was almost going to major in art history, but my love for solving problems and technology drove me to pursue a career in digital design. Currently I work for Gemalto as a UX Designer. I share nearly everything I find interesting on my Instagram and connect with my loved ones on Facebook. You can also find me networking on LinkedIn. If you have any questions or just want to say hi – email at hello@raquelburroughs.com.
I believe in finding context-driven design solutions highly sensitive to aesthetics and detail are paramount for delivering engaging, quality-oriented work. My approach is to embrace the entire design process (concept, strategy, implementation) in order to craft meaningful, measurable experiences for everyone.
