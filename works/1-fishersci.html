Fisher Scientific is a laboratory supply and biotechnology company that provided products and services to the global scientific research and United States clinical laboratory markets. Its customers included pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, colleges, universities, and secondary education institutions, medical research institutions, hospitals and reference labs and quality control, process control and research and development laboratories.


Minimum Viable Product

The user groups have completely opposite needs for the type of cross-sells displayed it’s critical that alternative products and supplementary products, are listed as separate cross-sell sections.

Having both supplementary and alternative products listed within the same section will not cater specifically to either group of users, and will in fact worsen the signal-to-noise ratio.


Experiment Setup

Provide easy access to alternative, supplementary, related products which will decrease bounce rate and increase page views.
Test 1: Effectiveness of the recommendation models
Test 2: Placement of the recommendation models
Test 3: With or without CTA (add to cart and price) on the recommendation models


Experiment Setup: Test 1

Test placement using models “Customers who viewed this also viewed”, "You may also like", and "Frequently bought together".
